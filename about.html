Our story

Founded in October 2025.

Dunixab Creative Studio began with a simple conviction: that great design should feel intentional in every dimension — visual, motion, spatial and sonic. From our base in Neyyattinkara, Trivandrum, we partner with brands who want more than decoration; they want craft that earns attention and drives business.

We're small by design, which means your project gets senior care from brief to delivery — and a team that treats your goals as our own.

A Message from Our CEO

When we started this company, we believed that great design is never just about how something looks—it's about how it works, how it feels, and how it connects with the people who experience it. Every project we take on is a partnership. We listen first, then we create. Our team brings together strategy, creativity, and craft to build designs that don't just meet expectations, but redefine them. Whether it's a brand identity, a digital product, or a complete visual experience, our goal remains the same: to deliver work that makes a real difference for our clients. I'm proud of the talented people behind every pixel, every concept, and every solution we produce. Their passion is the heartbeat of this company, and it's what allows us to turn ambitious ideas into reality. To our clients—thank you for trusting us with your vision. To those just discovering us—we'd love to show you what thoughtful, purposeful design can do. Let's create something remarkable together.

AKHIL R S
Founder & CEO
